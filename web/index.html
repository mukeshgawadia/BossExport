What We Do

Boss Export India is a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-quality Indian natural stones, including granite, marble, slate, sandstone, limestone and quartzite. We supply stones from North as well as South India. We, at Boss Export India, source different stones directly from quarries and process them at stone factory at Kisangarh, Rajasthan, India.



The processed natural stones are available in finishes like natural, honed, polished, calibrated, brushed, sandblasted and tumbled in hand-cut or machine-cut edges. Our company policy is to offer high-quality stones at affordable prices and impeccable customer-oriented services, so as to build and maintain healthy and long-term relationships with prospective and existing customers across the globe.
